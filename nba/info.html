Cc: Numerical Browsing Assistant (CC:NBA)

Info

Lets say you have the following files:
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj001.jpg
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj002.jpg
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj003.jpg
And you wish to view them in order with Cc:NBA
  1. Break the first file into three parts:
    • Prefix: "C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj"
    • Number "001"
    • Tail ".jpg"
  2. Paste them into their corisponding fields.
  3. Remove the leeding "0"s from the number
  4. Enter "3" in the digits section, and select the radio button beside that field.
  • To view the url press "Re".
  • To increase the number by N press "Plus".
  • To decrease the number by N press "Minus".
  • To increase the number by N every T deciseconds press "Slide =>".
  • To decrease the number by N every T deciseconds press "Slide <=".
  • To change the amount the number changes, change N.
  • To resize the frame, place your mouse on the right border, then drag the frame.

Lets say you have the follwoing files:
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj1.jpg
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj2.jpg
C:\mpcds\aacj\aacj3.jpg
Follow the same steps as above but, replace step 4 with:
  • Select "NA" from the digit selection.

*A decisecond is 1 / 10 of a second.
T is the number in the deciseconds field.
